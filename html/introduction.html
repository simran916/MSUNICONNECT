Features

Seat Booking

Scheduler for students where they can reserve the seat of their choice using wonderful virtual classroom portray.

Announcement Portal

Fully fuctional feature that allows Faculty to make an announcement from their dashboard and send it to the dashboard of all the students.

Database

The database of all the students accessible to the faculty (including the Vaccination Status).

PDF Generator

Generate the acceptance letter for eligible students in pdf format.

Responsive Design

Compatible with all the devices.

Covid-19 Precautions

Allowing the students to come offline only if they have taken both the shots of Vaccine.
